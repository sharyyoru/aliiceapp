# ALiice Medical CRM - Knowledge Base for Retell AI

## About ALiice

ALiice is a comprehensive medical CRM and clinic management platform designed specifically for aesthetics clinics, medical spas, and healthcare practices. It helps clinic owners manage patients, appointments, services, and business operations in one unified system.

### Target Users
- Clinic owners and administrators
- Medical practitioners (doctors, nurses, aestheticians)
- Front desk and reception staff
- Clinic managers

---

## Core Features

### 1. Patient Management
**What it does:** Store and manage all patient information including contact details, medical history, treatment records, and communication history.

**How to access:** Click "Patients" in the sidebar menu.

**Key capabilities:**
- Add new patients with contact information (name, email, phone)
- View complete patient profiles and history
- Search patients by name, email, or phone number
- Track patient lifecycle stages (lead, consultation, treatment, loyal)
- Merge duplicate patient records
- Import patients from CSV files
- Add medical notes and treatment history

**Common questions:**
- "How do I add a patient?" → Go to Patients, click the + Add Patient button, fill in details
- "Can I import my existing patients?" → Yes, use CSV import in the Patients section
- "How do I find a patient?" → Use the search bar at the top of the Patients page

### 2. Appointment Scheduling
**What it does:** Manage your clinic's calendar, book appointments, and track consultations.

**How to access:** Click "Calendar" or "Appointments" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- View daily, weekly, or monthly calendar
- Book appointments for patients
- Assign appointments to specific doctors/practitioners
- Set appointment duration and type
- Add appointment notes
- Send appointment confirmations via email
- Reschedule or cancel appointments
- View upcoming appointments on dashboard

**Common questions:**
- "How do I book an appointment?" → Go to Calendar, click on a time slot, select patient and service
- "Can patients book online?" → Yes, set up your booking page in CMS > Book Appointment
- "How do I reschedule?" → Click on the appointment, select Reschedule, choose new time

### 3. Services Catalog
**What it does:** Define all treatments and services your clinic offers with pricing and duration.

**How to access:** Click "Services" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- Add services with name, description, price, and duration
- Organize services into categories
- Set different prices for different practitioners
- Enable/disable services for online booking
- Track service popularity

**Common questions:**
- "How do I add a service?" → Go to Services, click Add Service, fill in details
- "Can I categorize treatments?" → Yes, create categories and assign services to them
- "How do I set pricing?" → Each service has a price field when you create or edit it

### 4. Online Booking Page
**What it does:** Create a customizable landing page where patients can book appointments directly.

**How to access:** Go to CMS > Book Appointment.

**Key capabilities:**
- Customize welcome message and branding
- Choose which services appear on booking page
- Select which practitioners are available for booking
- Set booking availability windows
- Get a shareable link for your website or social media
- Preview how patients see your booking page

**How to set up:**
1. Go to CMS in the sidebar
2. Click Book Appointment
3. Customize your page content and design
4. Click Copy Link to get your booking URL
5. Share the link with patients

**Common questions:**
- "How do I get my booking link?" → Go to CMS > Book Appointment, click Copy Link
- "Can I customize the design?" → Yes, use the visual editor to change colors, text, and layout
- "Which services appear on my booking page?" → Services you've enabled for online booking

### 5. Deals & Pipeline (CRM)
**What it does:** Track patient journey from initial inquiry to completed treatment using a sales pipeline.

**How to access:** Click "Deals" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- Create deals for potential treatments
- Move deals through pipeline stages
- Track deal value and expected revenue
- Assign deals to team members
- Set follow-up reminders
- View pipeline analytics

**Pipeline stages:**
1. New Lead - Initial inquiry received
2. Consultation Booked - Appointment scheduled
3. Consultation Done - Patient has been seen
4. Quote Sent - Treatment proposal provided
5. Won - Patient confirmed treatment
6. Lost - Patient declined

### 6. Financial Management
**What it does:** Handle invoicing, payments, and financial tracking.

**How to access:** Click "Financials" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- Create and send invoices
- Track payment status
- Accept online payments
- Generate financial reports
- Export data for accounting

**Common questions:**
- "How do I create an invoice?" → Go to Financials, click Create Invoice, select patient and services
- "Can patients pay online?" → Yes, configure payment gateway in Settings
- "How do I track revenue?" → View financial reports in the Financials section

### 7. Task Management
**What it does:** Assign and track tasks for team members.

**How to access:** Click "Tasks" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- Create tasks with due dates
- Assign tasks to team members
- Set task priorities
- Track task completion
- Link tasks to patients

### 8. Workflows & Automation
**What it does:** Automate repetitive tasks like follow-up emails and reminders.

**How to access:** Click "Workflows" in the sidebar.

**Key capabilities:**
- Create automated email sequences
- Set up appointment reminders
- Trigger actions based on patient activity
- Schedule follow-up communications

## Getting Started - Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Add Your First Patient
1. Click "Patients" in the sidebar
2. Click the "+ Add Patient" button
3. Enter patient details (name, email, phone)
4. Click Save

### Step 2: Set Up Your Services
1. Click "Services" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add Service"
3. Enter service name, duration, and price
4. Save and repeat for all treatments

### Step 3: Create Your Booking Page
1. Go to CMS > Book Appointment
2. Customize your welcome message
3. Enable services for online booking
4. Copy your booking link
5. Share on your website and social media

### Step 4: Schedule Your First Appointment
1. Click "Calendar" in the sidebar
2. Click on an available time slot
3. Select the patient and service
4. Add any notes
5. Save the appointment

### Step 5: Configure Settings
1. Go to "Settings" in the sidebar
2. Set up doctor scheduling (working hours)
3. Configure calendar defaults
4. Add blocked dates for holidays

### Step 6: Set Up Payments (Optional)
1. Go to "Financials" in the sidebar
2. Configure your payment gateway
3. Create your first invoice

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
- **Forgot password?** Click "Forgot Password" on login page to reset
- **Account locked?** Contact support for assistance
- **Browser issues?** Try clearing cache or using incognito mode

### Page Not Loading
- Refresh the page with Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser

### Data Not Saving
- Ensure all required fields are filled in
- Check for error messages at the top of the form
- Try refreshing and entering data again

### Appointment Issues
- Verify the patient exists in the system first
- Check that the time slot is available
- Ensure the practitioner is not blocked for that time

### Can't Find a Patient
- Try searching by email or phone number
- Check if patient was archived
- Verify spelling of patient name

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General
**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, ALiice uses enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage and secure connections.

**Q: Can I access ALiice on mobile?**
A: Yes, ALiice works on any device with a web browser. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon.

**Q: How many users can I have?**
A: User limits depend on your subscription plan. Contact support for details.

### Patients
**Q: Can I import patients from another system?**
A: Yes, you can import patients using CSV files. Go to Patients and look for the Import option.

**Q: How do I merge duplicate patients?**
A: Select the duplicate records in Patients, then click the Merge button.

**Q: Can patients access their own records?**
A: Patient portal features are coming soon.

### Appointments
**Q: Can I set recurring appointments?**
A: Yes, when creating an appointment you can set it to repeat.

**Q: How do patients receive confirmations?**
A: Email confirmations are sent automatically when appointments are booked.

**Q: Can I block time for lunch or meetings?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Blocked Dates to add time blocks.

### Billing
**Q: What payment methods are supported?**
A: Credit cards, bank transfers, and various payment gateways depending on your region.

**Q: Can I offer payment plans?**
A: Yes, you can create installment invoices in the Financials section.

### Technical
**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Click the "Talk to Aliice" button in the bottom right corner, or email support@aliice.app.
